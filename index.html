MihAddon v0.3

MihAddon is a San Andreas Multiplayer (SA:MP) modification which aims to improve the experience during gameplay.

It was made by Mihael, who after the blacklist of the popular modification "SAMP Addon" on a server he plays, decided to take matters into his own hands and make a copy with all of his faverouite features + some crashfixes.

Current mods/fixes in MihAddon:

  1. A BUNCH of crash fixes that plague SA:MP and GTA:SA
  2. Fastmap/Rapidmap
  3. A transparent background and not pausing in the escape menu
  4. Forcing the FPS to be unlimited and limiting it to 100FPS
  5. Fastloader - pretty much skips the SAMP Splash screen, making it faster to login.
  6. nocamfreeze - a mod that doesn't freeze your camera if the server does - same thing SAMP Addon had (UNTESTED)

JustMike (the creator) plans to make a bunch more fixes and modifications as time goes on.

Installation

MihAddon was only tested on modloader, so do the following: extract the 3 files, and put all 3 of them into modloader.

Notes:

Two mods (limitfps100.asi and blurthatshit.asi) are not made by JustMike, they are 3rd party mods that he was too lazy to make himself
